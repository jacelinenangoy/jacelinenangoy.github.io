Morning Coffee / 2023

Interactive Microgame.




A calm interactive which allows the player to make their own cup of coffee to start the day!
In the morning my favourite way of making my coffee is with a pour over/ drip method using a filter as it is a slow process and allows me to think in the moment. The process itself takes a while (around 2 mins depending on the coffee beans grind size) as the water has to go through the filter, which produces a different taste to a much quicker espresso style coffee!

Interactive Microgame.
